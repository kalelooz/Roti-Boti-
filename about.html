Our Story

A Passion for Purity, a Taste of Tradition.

A Vision for Healthy, Authentic Cuisine

Established in 2016, Roti & Boti was born from a passion to redefine Indian cuisine. Our founder's vision was to "cook and serve healthy, Indian-inspired takeout cuisine" that stands in delicious contrast to the traditionally heavy and oily dishes often found elsewhere. We believe that food can be both wholesome and rich in flavor, prepared with only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients.

Our Philosophy

Our mission is to offer an authentic yet innovative dining experience. We embrace locally sourced ingredients to create "wholesome, flavorful & authentic Asian cuisine particularly Indian / Mughlai, Afghan & Chinese". This commitment to quality is matched by our dedication to providing a fine dining atmosphere with "Efficient and friendly service."

The Roti & Boti Ambiance

Step into our "Upmarket, neat, well laid out Space" that radiates a "wonderful atmosphere". Our interiors and furniture are meticulously chosen to create an inviting and refined setting, making it the perfect place for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a professional luncheon.

Our Services

To meet all your needs, we offer a range of services including elegant dine-in, convenient takeaway & delivery, professional outdoor catering for personal and corporate events, and our famous all-you-can-eat Indian buffet on select days.
